SoundBall

The project

One of my biggest, advanced and ongoing projects yet. I wanted to investigate new approaches to sound design. I came up with the sound ball. It is a 3d printed sphere that transforms movement into sound. To manipulate the sound you can use the vst.

How the projects helped me develop my skills.

Figma was used to develop the prototype of the vst. While blender was used to develop the 3d model for the soundball. I had to use a 3D printer to make the physical prototype, which involved learing the slicing software used in 3D printing. For the functional part of the project I installed a microbit and a battery pack to power it inside the Soundball. The programm is still in development.
